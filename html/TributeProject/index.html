About Hedi Jouini

Hedi Jouini (1909–1990) was a Tunisian singer, oud player, and composer, often regarded as the father of Tunisian music.
His songs deeply influenced Arab music, blending Tunisian folklore with modern composition. With more than 1,000 songs and over 50 operettas,
his legacy remains timeless.

Career Timeline

  • 1909 - Born in Tunis, Tunisia.
  • 1936 - First major composition broadcast on Tunisian radio.
  • 1940s - Composed operettas and became a household name.
  • 1950s - Recognized as the father of Tunisian popular music.
  • 1990 - Passed away, leaving behind a legendary heritage.
