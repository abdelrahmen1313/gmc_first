About Us

At Los Pollos Hermanos, cooking isn’t just a job — it’s a ritual of patience, craft, and heart.
Our team finds joy in the rhythm of the kitchen, the careful mixing, the slow marination, the laughter that fills the air as spices dance in perfect balance.
We stay loyal to our roots, choosing authentic ingredients and time-tested methods over shortcuts and automation. While the industry races toward high-tech kitchens and synthetic flavors, we keep our hands in the dough and our hearts in the flame.
Every meal we serve is the result of tradition, teamwork, and genuine passion — the way good food was always meant to be made.
Welcome to Los Pollos Hermanos — where flavor is family.
